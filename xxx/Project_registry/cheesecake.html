Cheesecake

PREP TIME: 20 min.

TOTAL TIME: 5 hours 45 min. (incl. refrigerating)

MAKES: 16 servings

Ingredients

for cheesecake

15 graham crackers, crushed
2 tablespoons butter, melted
4 packages cream cheese (8 ounce) or ( 50% cream cheese : 50% cottage cheese )
1/2 cups white sugar
4 eggs
1 cup sour cream
1 tablespoon vanilla extract or 2 pack

Directions

for cheesecake

Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
Grease a 9 inch springform pan.
TIPS
HEAT

oven to 325F.

MIX

graham crumbs and butter; press onto bottom of 9-inch springform pan.

BEAT

cream cheese, sugar and vanilla with mixer until blended.
Add eggs, 1 at a time, mixing on low speed after each just until blended.
Pour over crust.

BAKE

55 min. or until center is almost set.
Loosen cake from rim of pan; cool * before removing rim.
Refrigerate 4 hours.

*Chill
